Recipe 2

Description

Recipe 2 is a delicious mochi recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mochiko (sweet rice flour)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 can sweetened red bean paste
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix mochiko, sugar, and baking powder together in a bowl.
  3. Add water and vanilla extract to the bowl; mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until top is golden brown, about 1 hour.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes
  7. Spread red bean paste over the top of the mochi.
  8. Cut into squares.

Enjoy!
